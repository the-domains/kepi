San Francisco based Kepi may be best known as the producer behind "Kepi and Kat", the electronic music duo voted Best Electronic Music Act in the Bay Area (SF Guardian). He has remixed for Danny Howells, collaborated with Desert Dwellers and produced events featuring Hybrid, Steve Porter, Luke Fair, Grandmaster Flash and The Crystal Method.
